Hop the Shortcut!

Am I killing all the grass?
Nope, I am just playing Hopscotch!

Change your everyday informal shortcut into a playful game.

01

Protocol of the Site

Protocol

The site is a patch of ground in front of N10. People use their own ‘shortcut’s, instead of the made path. Over time, repeated footsteps erased the grass and revealed the ground.

Efficiency isn't everything.

If we cannot revive the grass or prohibit people from using the shortcut, then JUST MAKE IT MEANINGFUL in another way!

02

Crafting Process

The logic worked; the material failed.

Prototype

Craft foams were too thin, weak, and light.
The ground was too stiff for the U-pin to be stuck in.

Building a stronger playable path.

Final Making

The final pieces were built in coated paper foam board.
